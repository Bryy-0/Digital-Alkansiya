Our You Can Create Campaign

Our You Can Create Campaign has been raising funds since 2025 to advance digital readiness, particularly through Project CLIMB (Create Learning in Mountain Barangays), our initiative under Create Library, dedicated to distributing literacy and numeracy materials, and eventually, digital tools, to remote upland communities. We started with physical cans and now it has evolved into our Digital Alkansiya for a more sustainable way of giving!
